ALIGNING DINALEDI

AIM

Aligning Dinaledi is a platform that creates hope for under privillege and middle class schooling children. The main aim of the orgnisation is to ensure th at all kids have text books, school shoes and food to eat at schoool as we are aware how muchh that can contribute to the learning experience

MISSION.

Our mission is to provide educational support to underprivileged learners, empowering them to overcome financial barriers and achieve academic excellence.

VISION.

Our vision is to create a world where every learner has access to quality education, regardless of their socio-economic background.
VALUES.
Integrity, Empathy, Collaboration, and Excellence. We believe in the power of education to transform lives and communities, and we are committed to making a positive impact in the lives of learners.
GOALS.
Our goals are to provide educational resources, mentorship, and support to underprivileged learners, helping them to achieve their academic and personal goals. We aim to create a sustainable impact in the communities we serve, fostering a culture of learning and empowerment.
